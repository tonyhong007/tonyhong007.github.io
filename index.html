I am passionate about building highly efficient machine learning systems and distributed systems.

I am an incoming M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I will be working with Minjia Zhang in the Supercomputing System AI Lab.

Recently, I was a visiting researcher in the Distributed and Storage Systems Lab at UIUC, working with Ram Alagappan and Aishwarya Ganesan on shared logs and vector databases. I was also an early team member of Reboot, a novel framework for building reactive backends out of durable data structures that safely compose into scalable distributed systems.

In my previous life, I was advised by Ion Stoica in the Sky Computing Lab at UC Berkeley, where I also worked closely with Yifan Qiao, Jaewan Hong, Frank Luan, and Benjamin Hindman on various topics, including GPU resource sharing, distributed remote memory, RPCs, shuffle systems, and Pub/Sub systems.
